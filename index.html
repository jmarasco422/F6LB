Manager Reports
Daily & Monthly District Manager Reports

Manager Reports give District Managers Parts & Service (DMPS) a real-time view of Focus 6 performance across every store in their district. Each report surfaces advisor-level data, store standings, and district leaderboard positions — all in a clean, printable PDF format.

What's in the report

  • Units sold per advisor, per F6 product
  • % to NADA Replacement Propensity Objective
  • Store leader callout per product
  • District position (anonymized to prevent cross-store data sharing)
  • Bar graphs showing performance vs. objective and district leader

Best practices

  • Print and post the PDF where all staff can see it to drive engagement
  • Recognize and champion Store Leaders and advisors meeting their F6 objectives
  • Use the report to coach advisors on lost revenue opportunities

Buy/Sells
Store Transition Procedures

When a dealership undergoes a buy/sell, F6LB data must be handled carefully to ensure accuracy and continuity. This section covers the procedures for managing store transitions within the F6LB system.

What to do during a buy/sell

  • Notify the F6LB administrator before the transition date
  • Archive the existing store file with the previous ownership data
  • Reset advisor email lists for the new ownership group
  • Coordinate with the incoming DMPS on data continuity preferences

Common questions

  • Historical data from the previous ownership is preserved separately
  • The new store file starts fresh from the transition date
  • District standings are not affected by buy/sell transitions

Unknown Advisors
Resolving Unmatched Advisor Entries

Unknown Advisors appear in the F6LB system when Maxis data contains a name or employee ID that doesn't match any preloaded advisor in the store file. Resolving these quickly keeps your leaderboard accurate and fair.

Common causes

  • New advisor not yet added to the store file
  • Name spelling mismatch between Maxis and the store file
  • Employee ID change or update in Maxis
  • Temp or floater advisor from another store

Resolution steps

  • Identify the unknown entry in the ADMIN tab
  • Cross-reference with the store's current roster in Maxis
  • Add or correct the advisor entry in the store file
  • Re-run the data load for that store
  • If unresolvable, submit a support ticket

The ADMIN Tab
Full Workbook Administration Guide

The ADMIN Tab is the control center of the F6LB workbook. Everything from loading daily data to pushing PDF reports to managing advisor email lists runs through here.

Core functions

  • Paste Maxis/Spiffy data into the Loader
  • Review auto-distributed store files for accuracy
  • Click the F6LB logo to trigger PDF generation and email delivery

"I Sold More"
Handling Leaderboard Disputes

Sometimes an advisor believes their leaderboard numbers don't reflect their actual sales. This section covers the process for investigating and resolving these disputes accurately and fairly.

Why discrepancies happen

  • Repair orders not yet closed in Maxis at time of data pull
  • Customer pay vs. warranty distinction in Maxis (only CP counts)
  • Sale attributed to wrong advisor in the RO
  • Data pull timing — yesterday's close vs. current month

How to handle it

  • Ask the advisor for the specific RO number(s) in question
  • Verify the RO is customer pay and closed in Maxis
  • Check the advisor name on the RO matches their F6LB entry
  • If confirmed discrepancy, submit a support ticket with RO details

Out of Office
Managing F6LB During Absences

When advisors or managers are away, F6LB can be configured to handle absences gracefully — keeping data clean and competition fair.

Advisor is out

  • Advisor's data simply won't accumulate while they're absent — this is expected
  • No action needed for short absences (1–2 days)
  • For extended leave, notify your DMPS so context is available for coaching

DMPS is out

  • Identify a backup contact to run the daily ADMIN tab send
  • Ensure backup has access to the workbook and email credentials
  • Alternatively, pause delivery for the absence period — submit a ticket to request

Analyst is out

  • Data load cannot proceed without the analyst — reports will be delayed
  • DMPS will be notified when a delay occurs
  • Reports will resume on the next available day automatically
